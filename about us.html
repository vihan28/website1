Jamnabai Narsee School is a private school in Juhu, Mumbai, Maharashtra, India.
It was founded on 17 January 1971 and is managed by the Narsee Monjee Education Trust.
The school building is unusual and unique in architecture with three clusters of hexagonal classrooms, each with a central foyer.
The school is attended by children from all over Mumbai, and its alumni include several famous dignitaries and celebrities.
